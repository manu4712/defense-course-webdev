Basic Nutrition Principles

  • Balanced Macronutrients: Include proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats in every meal.
  • Hydration: Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • Meal Timing: Eat every 3-4 hours to maintain energy levels
  • Portion Control: Use your hand as a guide - palm-sized protein, fist-sized vegetables, cupped-hand carbs

Pre-Workout Nutrition (30-60 minutes before)

  • Light Options: Banana with peanut butter, oatmeal with berries, or protein smoothie.
  • Avoid: Heavy meals, high-fat foods, or excessive fiber.

Post-Workout Nutrition (within 30 minutes)

  • Protein: 20-30 grams (protein shake, chocolate milk, or Greek yogurt).
  • Carbohydrates: Quick-digesting carbs to replenish energy stores.

Weight Loss Diet Tips

  • Caloric Deficit: Consume fewer calories than you burn.
  • High Protein: Aim for 0.8-1g protein per pound of body weight.
  • Vegetables: Fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables.
  • Avoid: Processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive snacking.

Muscle Gain Diet Tips

  • Caloric Surplus: Eat slightly more calories than you burn.
  • Protein Priority: 1-1.2g protein per pound of body weight.
  • Complex Carbs: Brown rice, quinoa, oats for sustained energy.
  • Healthy Fats: Nuts, olive oil, avocado for hormone production.
