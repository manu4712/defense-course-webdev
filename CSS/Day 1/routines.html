Beginner Workout Routine (3 Days/Week)

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3
Warm-up: 5-10 minutes light cardio Warm-up: 5 minutes arm circles and light stretching Warm-up: 5 minutes light walking
Squats: 3 sets of 8-12 reps Chest Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
Push-ups: 3 sets of 5-10 reps (modify as needed) Lat Pulldown: 3 sets of 8-12 reps Lunges: 3 sets of 8-12 per leg
Seated Rows: 3 sets of 8-12 reps Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
Plank: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-15 reps Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
Walking: 10-15 minutes cool down Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 10-15 reps Hamstring Curls: 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Advanced Workout Tips

  • Progressive Overload: Gradually increase weight, reps, or sets each week.
  • Proper Form: Always prioritize technique over heavy weight.
  • Rest Periods: 60-90 seconds between sets for beginners, 90-180 seconds for advanced.
  • Recovery: Allow 48 hours between training the same muscle groups.
