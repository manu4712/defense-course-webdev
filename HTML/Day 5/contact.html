Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you offer guest passes?

A: Yes, members receive 2 guest passes per month with Premium and VIP memberships.


Q: Is there parking available?

A: Yes, we have free parking for all members and visitors.


Q: Do you have locker rooms?

A: Yes, we have separate men's and women's locker rooms with showers and lockers.


Q: Can I freeze my membership?

A: Yes, memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel.
