General Gym Safety Rules

Before You Start

  • Medical Clearance: Consult your doctor before beginning any exercise program.
  • Proper Attire: Wear appropriate workout clothes and closed-toe athletic shoes.
  • Hydration: Bring a water bottle and stay hydrated throughout your workout.
  • Warm-up: Always spend 5-10 minutes warming up before exercising.

Equipment Safety

  • Learn Proper Form: Ask trainers for instruction on new exercises.
  • Use Spotters: Have someone assist you with heavy weights, especially on bench press and squats.
  • Safety Clips: Always use safety clips/collars on barbells to secure weight plates.
  • Weight Limits: Don't exceed your strength limits - progress gradually.

Gym Etiquette

  • Wipe Down Equipment: Clean machines and benches after use.
  • Re-rack Weights: Return all weights and equipment to their proper places.
  • Share Equipment: Allow others to "work in" during your rest periods.
  • Personal Space: Give others adequate room to exercise safely.
  • Phone Use: Keep phone conversations brief and away from equipment.

Emergency Procedures

  • Injury Protocol: Stop exercising immediately if you feel pain.
  • Staff Assistance: Alert gym staff for any injuries or equipment malfunctions.
  • First Aid: Know the location of first aid kits and emergency exits.
  • Emergency Contacts: Keep emergency contact information updated.

Health Guidelines

  • Stay Home When Sick: Don't come to the gym if you have symptoms of illness.
  • Cover Cuts: Protect any open wounds with bandages.
  • Shower Shoes: Wear flip-flops in locker rooms and shower areas.
  • Personal Items: Keep personal belongings secure and don't share towels or water bottles.
